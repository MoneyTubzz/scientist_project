Dr. Michio Kaku, PhD


Michio Kaku, born on January 24, 1947, is a renowned theoretical physicist futurist, and professor at the City College of New York, where he co-founded string field theory. Known for making complex scientific ideas accessible and engaging, he has authored several bestselling books, including Physics of the Impossible and The Future of the Mind. Kaku also hosts the radio program "Science Fantastic" and frequently appears on television, podcasts, and documentaries, where he discusses topics such as the future of technology, space exploration, the nature of the universe, quantum computing, and so much more!
